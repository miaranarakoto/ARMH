About me

I'm Miarana Rakotomalala, from Madagascar. I was doing interior design first and now I'm a fresh student at university of Politecnico di Milano.
Since my young age, I was absorbed in the drawing world and later on, in the world of design.
In this website, I want to share a personal approach about creativity and art that I did since I draw seriously.
